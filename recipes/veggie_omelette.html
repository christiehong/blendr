Veggie Omelette

Difficulty:

Easy

Prep Time:

15 mins

Calories:

400 calories

Servings:

4 servings

Ingredients:

  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1 chopped red bell pepper
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1 box of mushrooms
  • 1 bag of baby spinach leaves
  • 8 eggs
  • ½ tsp of salt
  • ½ tsp of pepper

Directions:

  1. In a nonstick skillet, heat oil over medium-high heat. Add bell pepper, onion and mushrooms to oil. Cook 2 minutes, stirring frequently, until onion is tender. Stir in spinach; continue cooking and stirring just until spinach wilts. Remove vegetables from pan to small bowl.
  2. In medium bowl, beat egg product, water, salt and pepper with fork or whisk until well mixed. Reheat same skillet over medium-high heat. Quickly pour egg mixture into pan. While sliding pan back and forth rapidly over heat, quickly stir with spatula to spread eggs continuously over bottom of pan as they thicken. Let stand over heat a few seconds to lightly brown bottom of omelet. Do not overcook; omelet will continue to cook after folding.
  3. Place cooked vegetable mixture over half of omelet; garnish with more salt and pepper. With spatula, fold other half of omelet over vegetables. Gently slide out of pan onto plate. Serve immediately.
