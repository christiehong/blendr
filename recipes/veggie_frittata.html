Spinach Potato Frittata

Difficulty:

Easy

Prep Time:

30 mins

Calories:

250 calories

Servings:

8 servings

Ingredients:

  • 6 small red potatoes
  • 2 tbsp of olive oil
  • 1 bag of spinach
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 onion
  • 6 eggs
  • ½ tsp of salt
  • ½ tsp of pepper
  • ½ cup of milk
  • 1 cup Cheddar cheese

Directions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a medium skillet over medium heat. Place potatoes in the skillet, cover, and cook about 10 minutes, until tender but firm. Mix in spinach, onion, and garlic. Season with salt and pepper. Continue cooking 1 to 2 minutes, until spinach is wilted.
  2. n a medium bowl, beat together eggs and milk. Pour into the skillet over the vegetables. Sprinkle with Cheddar cheese. Reduce heat to low, cover, and cook 5 to 7 minutes, or until eggs are firm.
